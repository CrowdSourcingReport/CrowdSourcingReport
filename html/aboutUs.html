Idea Behind LetsSankalp.Com


Don't know whether your donation is in right hands? Confused regarding selection of NGO? Don't worry. You are at right place! We provide a complete solution that suits your social serving needs. CSR is a platform benificial for both investors and donators.

For Donors

  • Be assured about the credibility of NGO
  • Easy Payment and registration process
  • Find right NGO to support your social serving needs

For NGOs

  • Showcase your project to the world through us
  • Get funded quickly
  • Provide Progress report to donors through our compact and easy-to-use tool

We consider the trust built in us over the time as most valuable and thus we showcase only those NGOs verified by us.
